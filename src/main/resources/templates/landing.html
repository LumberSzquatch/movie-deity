Movie Deity

A movie watchlist manager for indecisive film junkies

About Movie Deity


Movie Deity is for avid movie watchers that have an overflowing backlog of movies they want to watch. Easily create a no commitment account and start adding those movies to a "watchlist". When you're in the mood for something to watch, but not really sure, let Movie Deity decide. You can choose for it to pick a movie at random from your entire watchlist, or choose a genre and let pick one at random from there.


Needing a solution to the back and forth the creator and his wife would go through when choosing a movie for movie night, Movie Deity was spawned. A genre was something easy to agree on, but a specific movie? That's a tough cookie. Now when a movie is chosen at random, the only thing at blame is lines of code.


Why Does This Exist?


THE WHY: The primary goal behind this simple application is for the designer, and also developer (contact info below) to gain experience and a deeper understanding with the technologies he is familiar with, along with picking up a few more along the way. Those technologies being, SpringBoot as the MVC framework, Spring Security, Thymeleaf as the templating language, and of course JavaScript (with JQuery) and CSS, all deployed and managed on Heroku.


THE LONG TERM: Basically no different from the primary goal of this application. The future of this application is dependent on the pursuit of knowledge. Hopefully the pursuit of knowledge is never lacking, and as long as it isn't, this application will hopefully see enhancements and overhauls.
